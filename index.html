About Me

Specifically I'm a big fan of the NBA because I grew up playing basketball, although you might not be able to tell if you met me in person becasue, well, let's just say I'm vertivally challenged. Due to that fact, I had to develop other interests outside of basketball. I watch a lot of movies and I especially love going to the theatre with my friends. I also listen to a lot of music. I'm mostly into hip-hop, but I like to think my taste is pretty ecclectic.
